about jfs

Jewish Family Services of Ottawa (JFS) is a full service, non-sectarian agency serving residents of the greater Ottawa area – located at 2255 Carling Avenue, Suite 300.
We offer more than 65 programs and services to children, youth, adults and seniors, be they individuals, couples, families or groups.
